Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is ShivaChat?

A: ShivaChat is a spiritual app where users can engage in text and voice chats with AI personas of Hindu deities such as Lord Shiva, Ganesha, Krishna, Lakshmi, and others. It also includes guided meditations and access to holy scriptures.

Q: How do I start a chat with a deity?

A: Simply open the app and select the deity you'd like to connect with. You can choose text or voice mode and begin your conversation.

Q: Are the responses from deities accurate or religiously verified?

A: The responses are generated by AI and inspired by Hindu philosophy and scriptures. They are designed to provide reflection and guidance, not formal religious advice.

Q: Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

A: Yes, internet is required for real-time AI processing and access to content like meditations and scriptures.

Q: Can I use ShivaChat offline?

A: Certain features such as saved meditations may be accessible offline in future updates, but real-time chat currently requires a connection.
